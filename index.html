Who We Are

Beyond Tomorrow Studios is a small independent studio bringing curiosity and play to the world. Cozy games are at the heart of what we do — and from there we make interactive prototypes, exhibitions and digital products for brands, broadcasters and cultural institutions.

We started as a cozy game studio, and Sleep Swimmer is still our flagship — but we're not only a games studio. We're a small team that brings interactivity, gamification and play to the world outside of games. If a fictional idea needs a working prototype, a brand needs a play-shaped campaign, or a venue needs an installation that feels alive: we'd love a chat.

Sleep
Swimmer

A 2D cozy adventure puzzle · PC · Coming soon

Aria is overwhelmed by the demands of modern life. When she goes to sleep she escapes to Salamanderville — a dreamy underwater town full of salamanders, jellyfish and (eventually) whales. Befriend the locals, collect sounds from around town and remix them on your DJ set to help solve their problems and weave a relaxing melodic score for Aria's dream.

The Longest Night

Tangibility / Worlds Colliding.

An exhibition of physical bridges into displaced worlds. Loosely inspired by the prologue of Ursula K. Le Guin's Rocannon's World — and the artefact in it that connects distant worlds — we're looking for tangible pieces you can touch, hold, or move through; pieces that suggest an entire elsewhere.

The Longest Night lives inside an active, social nighttime environment, so work needs to hold its own with that energy. Open to existing pieces and new proposals. Venue and dates shared on selection.
