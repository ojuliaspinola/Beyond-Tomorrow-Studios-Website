Who We Are

Beyond Tomorrow Studios is an independent cozy game studio in London, making narrative-driven games for everyone — accessible enough to play for two minutes, deep enough to spend ten hours in.

We met at the National Film and Television School and started Beyond Tomorrow in 2023 to go all-in on Sleep Swimmer — Julia's NFTS grad project, which won the audience over at EGX 2022 and never let go. Our number-one priority is escape: that's why we play games, and it's why we make them.

Sleep
Swimmer

A 2D cozy adventure puzzle · PC · Coming soon

Aria is overwhelmed by the demands of modern life. When she goes to sleep she escapes to Salamanderville — a dreamy underwater town full of salamanders, jellyfish and (eventually) whales. Befriend the locals, collect sounds from around town and remix them on your DJ set to help solve their problems and weave a relaxing melodic score for Aria's dream.

The Team

Julia Spinola

Co-founder · Game Designer & Director

Julia leads design and direction at Beyond Tomorrow. She's an NFTS graduate, and her producer credits include Tales of Seikyu and Duskfade. Sleep Swimmer started life as her NFTS grad project.

Harry Lee

Co-founder · Creative Producer & Biz Dev

Harry leads production, partnerships and business development. NFTS graduate; television producer credits include Building the Band (Netflix) and The Finish Line (BBC).
